    My academic journey


    From a young age, I have always been passionate about technology and problem-solving. This interest led me to excel in my GCE Ordinary Level exams with 9A passes, and later, I chose the Mathematics stream for my GCE Advanced Levels, laying a strong foundation in logical thinking and analytical skills.

    Building on this, I pursued a BSc in Information Systems at the University of Sabaragamuwa, where I developed a deep understanding of how technology can support business processes and decision-making. To complement my technical knowledge, I also completed an HND in Business Management and a Diploma in Information Technology at Esoft Metro Campus, Pearson College, London, which helped me gain practical skills in management, IT, and business strategy.

    Through this combination of academic excellence and practical learning, I have been able to bridge the gap between technology and business, preparing myself to take on professional challenges in marketing, project management, and digital solutions.

    I have a strong academic background in information systems, business management, and IT. My qualifications include:

    • BSc in Information Systems – University of Sabaragamuwa, Sri Lanka

    • Higher National Diploma (HND) in Business Management – Esoft Metro Campus, Pearson College, London

    • Diploma in Information Technology – Esoft Metro Campus, Pearson College, London

    • GCE Advanced Level (A/L) – Mathematics stream

    • GCE Ordinary Level (O/L) – 9A passes

    These qualifications provide me with a solid foundation in both technical and business disciplines, enabling me to manage projects, analyze systems, and lead strategic initiatives effectively.
